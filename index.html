The Story

Furdben Cards started the way the best things always do: genuine love for the hobby and sports. What began as a personal collection grew into a full-fledged operation spanning baseball, basketball, football, and soccer cards from every era.

The mission is simple: keep the hobby fun, accessible, and honest. Whether you're hunting a vintage rookie, completing a set, or just looking to flip some duplicates, Furdben is the kind of dealer you can actually trust.

Catch the action live on Whatnot, browse the full catalog on eBay, or follow along on Instagram for the latest pickups, pulls, and trade bait. And if you want to deal, just reach out. The door is always open.

"Every card tells a story. My job is to make sure it finds the right collector."

— Furdben Cards
